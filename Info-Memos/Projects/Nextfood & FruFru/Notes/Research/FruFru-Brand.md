# FruFru — Istorie Brand

## Origini
- **Fondat: 2006** de Mihai Simiuc
- Pozitionat ca **primul concept de fresh healthy food din Romania**
- Operat sub compania **Good People SA** (fosta **Mondo di Pasta SRL**, infiintata 2005)
- Produse: smoothies, salad bowls, supe, ready-to-eat food cu ingrediente proaspete
- Lider in segmentul premium food retail din Romania
- Hashtag brand: **#farabazaconii** (fara conservanti/prostii)

## Urban Monkey (brand sora)
- Tot fondat de Mihai Simiuc in 2006
- Focus: sucuri raw, cold-pressed si smoothies — nimic adaugat, niciodata incalzite
- Vandut la Unilever impreuna cu FruFru; cumparat inapoi impreuna in ianuarie 2024

## FruFru Cooperativa (entitate separata)
- Concept: branduri independente cu valori similare sub acelasi acoperis fizic
- Locatii: Bucuresti (Nicolae Titulescu 4-8 / America House; Piata Presei Libere 3-5 / City Gate), Cluj-Napoca (The Office), Timisoara (Str. Coriolan Brediceanu 10)
- Oferte: salate, supe, sandwich-uri, specialty coffee, sucuri proaspete
- **IMPORTANT: Cooperativele FruFru NU au fost incluse in tranzactia de cumparare inapoi de la Unilever — raman separate**

## Prezenta Retail Istorica (pre-Unilever)
FruFru a operat outlet-uri/corners in:
- Magazine **Mega Image**
- Locatii **Inmedio**
- Supermarketuri **Billa**
- Coridoare cladiri de birouri
- Spatii corporate
- Fresh corner displays

## Timeline Completa

```
2005  — Mondo di Pasta SRL fondat
2006  — FruFru si Urban Monkey create
2014  — Magazin online DETOX lansat (octombrie)
2016  — Redenumire in Good People SA; emisiune obligatiuni RON 4.55M; Revenue RON 22.5M
2018  — Revenue RON 34.4M; ~120 angajati
2019  — Unilever achizitioneaza 75% (iulie); deal finalizat (octombrie)
~2022 — Unilever cumpara restul de 25% de la Simiuc
2024  — Unilever vinde FruFru + Urban Monkey inapoi la Simiuc (ianuarie)
2024  — FruFru relansat in magazinele OTOTO (octombrie)
2024  — Simiuc ia 17.5% in LifeBox pentru productie (octombrie)
```

## Tranzactia Unilever — Detalii

### Faza 1: Unilever achizitioneaza FruFru (2019)

| Detaliu | Valoare |
|---------|---------|
| Data anunt | 4-5 iulie 2019 |
| Cumparator | Unilever |
| Tinta | Good People SA (FruFru + Urban Monkey) |
| Participatie achizitionata | 75% majoritar |
| Simiuc a pastrat | 25%, a continuat ca director general |
| Investitori care au iesit | Sergiu Negut, Philipp Hausmaninger, Johann Heubach, Maria Brujan, Marius Gavril |
| Aprobare regulatorie | Consiliul Concurentei Romania |
| Pret tranzactie | Nedivulgat public |
| Revenue la momentul tranzactiei (2018) | RON 34.4 mil |
| Angajati | ~120 |
| Finalizare | Aprobat si finalizat octombrie 2019 |

**Declaratia Unilever**: "Unilever is very excited to partner with and invest in FruFru, a beloved local foods business. The values and mission of FruFru will remain unchanged, and Mihai Simiuc will continue to lead the business while drawing on Unilever's experience and expertise."

**Context**: Unilever cumparase anterior producatorul roman de inghetata Betty Ice (2018). Parte din strategia Unilever de achizitii wellness/healthy food.

### Faza 2: Unilever devine actionar unic (~2022)
- La ~2 ani dupa achizitia initiala, Unilever a cumparat restul de 25%
- Unilever a devenit actionar unic Good People SA / FruFru

### Faza 3: Unilever vinde brandurile inapoi (ianuarie 2024)

| Detaliu | Valoare |
|---------|---------|
| Data anunt | 15-16 ianuarie 2024 |
| Vanzator | Unilever |
| Cumparator | Mihai Simiuc (fondator original) |
| Branduri vandute | FruFru si Urban Monkey |
| Durata detinere | ~5 ani |
| Pret tranzactie | Nedivulgat public |
| Motiv declarat | "Following a detailed strategic analysis" |
| EXCLUSE din deal | Unitatea de productie; Cooperativele FruFru |

### Faza 4: Relaunch (octombrie 2024)
- FruFru relansat in magazinele OTOTO ca brand independent
- Productia asigurata prin participatia de 17.5% in LifeBox (via Next Food)
